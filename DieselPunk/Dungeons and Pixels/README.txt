DUNGEONS & PIXELS — Premium Starter Pack

Overview

Dungeons & Pixels is a pixel-art asset pack (32x32) designed for prototyping and developing games in RPG, roguelike, dungeon crawler, action-adventure and similar genres.  

It includes dungeon tiles, props, characters, enemies, items and interactive objects. Compatible with Unity, Godot, GameMaker, Tiled, and other 2D engines.  

---

Content
- Dungeon Tileset (floors, walls, connectors, details).  
- Props 
  - Animated: torch, magic torch, trap, pressure plate, golden and silver sconces. 
  - Static: 2 types of crates, barrel, 2 types of debris, 3 types of chests (open/closed), 2 types of doors (open/closed), 2 types of cobwebs, 2 types of bookshelfs, closet, 2 types of tables.  
- Items  
  - Health potion, Mana potion.  
  - 3 types of keys and 3 types of locks. 
  - Gold and Silver coins. 
- Characters  
  - Player (Warrior) — 3 directions (front, side, back).  
  - Animations: Idle, Attack, Run, Death.  
- Enemies  
  - Skeleton warrior (3 directions).  
  - Skeleton archer (3 directions).  
  - Slime, Rat, Ghost, Spider (1 direction).  
  - Animations: Idle, Attack, Move, Death.  
- Each character includes a static shadow.  

---

Technical Info
- Tile size: 32x32 px  
- Format: PNG  
- Tiled support: `.tsx` tilesets and objects included  
- Unity / Godot: import-ready sprite sheets
